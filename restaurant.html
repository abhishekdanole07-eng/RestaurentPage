Our Story

A journey of passion, tradition, and culinary innovation

Founded in 2015 by Chef Marcus Aurelius, Savor & Sage began as a small bistro with a big dream: to create an unforgettable dining experience that honors traditional techniques while embracing modern innovation.

Our team of world-class chefs sources ingredients from local organic farms and international specialty suppliers, ensuring every dish meets our exacting standards of quality and sustainability.

Today, Savor & Sage stands as a beacon of culinary excellence, having earned two Michelin stars and the hearts of food lovers from around the globe.
